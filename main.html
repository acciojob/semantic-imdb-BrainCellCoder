Summary

"The Shawshank Redemption" is a 1994 American drama film directed by Frank Darabont, based on the Stephen King novella "Rita Hayworth and Shawshank Redemption." The film stars Tim Robbins and Morgan Freeman.

Actors

The main actors in the movie are Tim Robbins and Morgan Freeman. Tim Robbins plays the role of Andy Dufresne, while Morgan Freeman portrays Ellis Boyd "Red" Redding.
